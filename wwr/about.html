History

Founded with a love for wild rivers and safe adventure, our company began as a small team of guides helping friends experience the thrill of whitewater. Today we host guests from around the world.

Over the years we’ve expanded our routes and improved our equipment while staying true to our mission: safety, fun, and unforgettable memories on the river.
